Michelle Los is a Toronto based Eyebrow and Makeup Artist, specializing in eyebrow threading services. Born in London, Ontario, Michelle’s educational pursuits reflect her career aspirations and true life pleasures: she excelled in fine art studies in high school, earned a BA in Media/Communications Studies with a minor in English at Western University, and honed her makeup skills at the College of Makeup Art & Design. Her 20 years of work experience spans the hospitality, retail, media and beauty industries, resulting in a deep appreciation for client experience and a strong dedication to creating beautiful art for all ages, sexes and races. Throughout her career as a Makeup & Eyebrow Artist, Michelle has been privileged to work with dynamic brands like Jean Machine, Schwarzkopf and MAC Cosmetics. As a Brow Artist and eventually the Brow Director at Gee Beauty Toronto, Michelle developed department and training protocol & literature, flexed her creativity with studio schematics and fostered a strong and positive work environment.

Michelle is now offering services from the comfort of her private studio in the Wallace & Geary Ave neighbourhood of Toronto.

Services

Threading: Whether it’s to refine and polish your eyebrows, or add that extra freshness to your cheeks & jawline, Michelle delivers a threading experience that is surprisingly gentle and extremely effective.

When styling eyebrows, Michelle’s focus is to maintain a beautiful, natural brow that gives immediate gratification but also transcends throughout the years. Now that brows have the attention of the world, Michelle guides her clients through the process of resetting their brow growth and achieving their ideal style. Hair growth, face shape and client lifestyle are all taken into consideration and utilized to their full potential.

“I’m here to build your BEST brow game. When you visit my studio, we’ll co-design your look before I begin creating your ideal brow base. Our journey to frame your face with beautiful brows will continue over time, we’re in this together“

Facial threading creates a beautiful, smooth finish to the skin and prevents hair from growing back thicker. Michelle works with each client individually to determine how to best treat their skin before & after every session, and to ensure the greatest comfort and best results throughout the process.

Eyebrow & Eyelash Tinting: There’s no better way to highlight a fresh brow clean up than with an eyebrow tint. Michelle customizes every tint formulation to ensure that the final result is cohesive with her clients’ natural colouring. For clients who are growing out their eyebrows, Michelle utilizes tinting to strengthen the look of new hairs and build an updated brow shape.

And for those who struggle with wearing mascara, Michelle offers lash tinting to strengthen and pop her clients natural lashes.

Makeup: As a makeup artist, Michelle uses a modest touch and an edited selection of cosmetics to polish her clients’ look. She focuses on highlighting and celebrating natural features and building confidence where her clients feel challenged. Michelle is passionate about bringing out the inner makeup artist in everyone through technique instruction, makeup bag overhauls and seasonal change ups.
