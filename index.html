Welcome to Michelle Los beauty.

You must be here because you know the power of a great brow. Or, maybe you’re seeking a flawless makeup application that’s elegant and refined, like you. You’re in the right place.

At Michelle Los, we believe that beauty goes way beyond the purely aesthetic. Our relationship with beauty is dynamic, personal, and sometimes emotional. It touches the core of our confidence and inspires how we move through the world.

Your time with Michelle is your time to chill and practice some self-care. Sometimes a good chat is just what the doctor ordered. Sometimes silence is the best medicine. Michelle is dedicated to making your time in the studio your time, whatever that looks like.

Services

Michelle Los was created to bring you the very best brow and makeup experience. Relax and unwind in our state of the art studio, and leave looking and feeling confident, relaxed and refreshed.

Brows:
The Michelle Los brow experience begins with a co-design session. Together, we’ll discover your ideal brow base for your unique face and eye shape combination.

Next, Michelle will shape your brows to perfection, creating the best brow for your own unique face shape. With a natural gift for bringing out each client’s natural beauty, Michelle helps you bring your best brow game every day.

The journey to frame your face with beautiful brows will continue over time, we’re in this together.

Makeup:
As a makeup artist, Michelle uses a modest touch and a carefully curated selection of cosmetics to polish your look to perfection. Michelle Los helps you bring your best and most radiant natural self to any occasion.
